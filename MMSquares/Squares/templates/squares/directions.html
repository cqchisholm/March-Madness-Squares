How To Play

Click here to begin selecting squares. Input your initials into any blank square. The number of squares you should select depends on how many you want to buy in for. Speak with the manager of the game for details.

Once you have picked your squares your job is done. Sit back and enjoy watching the greatest sporting event of the year. Right before the tournament starts, numbers 0-9 will be randomly generated and placed horiztonally and vertically on the grid. Winning boxes are determined by looking at the final score of each game. The top row of numbers (horizontal) represents the winning team while the side numbers (vertical) represents the losing team.

For example, if Oregon beats Duke 75-69, Oregon is the winning team and the last digit of their score (75) is 5. We would look for the 5 across the top of the grid. Duke's score was 69, so we look for the 9 down the left side of the grid. The person with the square associated with '5' and '9' is the winner of that game.

Each round is worth more than the previous round. The manager of your pool can decide the exact amounts. Your scores will be automatically updated. You can view everyone's round by round score and total score in the scores table.
